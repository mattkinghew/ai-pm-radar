# Content Repurposing Template

## Purpose

This template turns one source into multiple reusable content outputs for AI PM, IP building, digital marketing, consulting, and portfolio case generation.

Use this for public sources, cleaned notes, anonymized interviews, approved client material, AI PM Radar articles, screenshots, PDFs, transcripts, course notes, or activity reports.

Do not use raw private data, credentials, client secrets, participant details, or full paid course content.

### LinkedIn Post

```text
Opening hook:
A useful AI workflow is not about replacing human judgment. It is about designing where human judgment must stay.

Context:
When working with AI-assisted research, content, and project documentation, the risky part is often not drafting. The risky part is publishing unchecked output or exposing sensitive context.

What I noticed:
Small teams often jump from “AI can summarize this” to “AI can handle the workflow”. That creates accuracy, privacy, and stakeholder alignment risks.

Why it matters:
For AI PM and digital transformation work, the real value is not only tool usage. It is workflow design.

AI PM / consulting insight:
A safer workflow separates source capture, AI-assisted synthesis, human review, and final approval.

Practical takeaway:
Before using AI on client-facing work, define what can be uploaded, what must be anonymized, and what requires human sign-off.

Limitation / risk:
This does not replace legal, compliance, or organizational policy review.

CTA:
I am documenting this as part of my AI PM Radar workflow system.
```
