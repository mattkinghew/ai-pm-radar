# Landing Page / Prototype Brief

## Purpose

This template helps plan a landing page or lightweight demo prototype for AI PM, IP building, digital marketing, and digital transformation consulting work.

It is designed for early validation and stakeholder alignment. It should not be presented as a finished production system unless implementation, QA, privacy, analytics, and maintenance have been separately planned.

Competitor pages or reference products may be used for structure learning, positioning comparison, and pattern observation only. Do not copy their text, layout, visuals, brand assets, screenshots, code, claims, or proprietary structure directly.

```text

AI-assisted tasks:
- summarize source notes

- generate FAQ candidates
- identify claim risks
- produce low-fidelity copy draft

Human review:
- confirm business accuracy
- confirm privacy handling
- confirm copyright safety

- confirm CTA and data collection
```

Suggested workflow:

```text
Source notes → AI-assisted brief draft → human review → landing page outline → copy draft → risk check → prototype plan
```

# Example: Generic AI Workflow Audit Landing Page

```text
Product / service name:
Client-safe AI Workflow Audit

Target audience:
SME, NGO, and education teams using AI for content, proposals, or project documentation.

Problem statement:
Small teams want to use AI to save time, but they lack clear rules for what can be uploaded, what must be reviewed, and what should never be automated.

Value proposition:
A lightweight audit that maps current workflow, identifies safe AI opportunities, and recommends a 7-day MVP without over-automation.

Page sections:
1. Hero: Use AI in client-facing work without losing control of quality and privacy.
2. Problem: AI tools are easy to try but hard to govern.
3. Who this is for: SME, NGO, education, consultant, personal brand teams.
4. Workflow: audit → safe sample → MVP workflow → review checklist.
5. Deliverables: workflow map, AI opportunity list, risk checklist, 7-day roadmap.
6. Proof: generic sample workflow and portfolio case note.
7. Risk note: not legal or compliance advice.
8. CTA: Request a workflow audit.

Competitor reference notes:
Use consulting and SaaS landing pages only to study section order and CTA placement. Do not copy their wording, layout, claims, or visuals.

AI-assisted workflow:
Sanitized notes → AI-assisted brief → human review → original copy draft → risk check.

Next action:
Prepare a one-page wireframe and confirm the CTA.
```
